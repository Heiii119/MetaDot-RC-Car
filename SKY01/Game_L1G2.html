🏎️ F = ma Racing Car Game

Move the sliders and watch the car. The app shows that net force = driving force − friction, and a bigger net force gives a bigger acceleration.

Controls

Push from the engine.
More mass means harder to speed up.
Friction: 6 N
Friction acts backward, so it is deducted first.
Net Force = Driving Force − Friction
24 N − 6 N = 18 N
Acceleration = Net Force ÷ Mass
18 N ÷ 8 kg = 2.25 m/s²
Set the sliders, then press Start.
